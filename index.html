Injuries happen all around the world. Some injuries can be severe and could effect the way you live your life and some just leave a little mark that will heal after some time. Either way you put it no one likes injuries at all it just ruins your life until it gets healed. Athletes get injured very often. They are playing various sports that require a lot of contact and movement. It is proven that approximately 21 percent of brain injuries among american children are from sports. People never know when someone could get hurt playing the sport or just when there is something wrong with there body health like heart rate and blood pressure and things that you could not just see by looking at someone. So what I decided to do was think of ways that I can tell when a person is in danger or when they are about to get injured. I thought of making a system of devices and cameras that will keep track of players biometrics and their physical state. I brought together 3 different devices. All of these devices have their own job. They will help gather this important information on the players and store it into one single computer. We will be able to detect when soemone is having problems with in their bodys and stop them from playing before anything tragic happens.

This is very important because if someones blood pressure is too high it could cause headaches and difficult breathing which is very important when being so active. If someones heart rate gets to high they will get light headed and feel dizzy and they could possibly faint.
